Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. What Information Is Collected

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when submitting forms, requesting offers, or contacting us. This may include your name, phone number, email address, property information, and any details relevant to your property or transaction.

2. How User Data Is Used

Your information is used to evaluate your property, communicate with you, provide offers, improve our services, and fulfill legal and operational requirements.

3. SMS Opt-In Details

By providing your phone number and submitting a form, you consent to receive SMS messages from Cedar Acquisitions regarding your inquiry, property offers, and related updates. Message frequency may vary. Standard message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

4. Cookie & Tracking Practices

We may use cookies, tracking pixels, and analytics tools to enhance user experience, analyze website performance, and improve marketing efforts.

5. Data Security and Handling

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

6. User Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.

7. Mobile Information Sharing Statement

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

8. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with legal advisors, title companies, lenders, and service providers necessary to complete transactions.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.
